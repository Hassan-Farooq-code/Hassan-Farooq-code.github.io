About Randago

Greenbins Randago Enterprises is a Garbage collection, Fumigation and Cleaning Services Company located in Kakamega County with comprehensive experience and skilled environmental professionals, we provide high quality service teamed with a network of highly recogised disposal facilities, to offer efficient, cost-effective, environmentally sound solutions to Garbage collection, Fumigation and Cleaning.
Randago has built its business on careful planning, depth in technical management, and a corporate structure that promotes the reliability and successful completion of customer assignments.
