Sanitary Pads Collection & Disposal

Flushing feminine hygiene products down the toilet will pollute rivers and block sewer lines posing a health hazard to both town dwellers and rural citizens. Our company handles female hygiene pads in a safe, sensitive and environmentally friendly. We offer a choice of various sanitary bin sizes: Small (8-10 litres), Medium (18 Litres), Large (20Litres), Granules (1Kg) suitable for corporates, residential premises, offices, hospitals, apartments, hotels/restaurants/clubs. Business premises, commercial buildings and homes at very competitive prices.

Safe Sanitary Pads Collection and Disposal

Our staff shall be equipped with yellow bags with black strip used to store non-hazardous human hygiene waste which shall then be properly labeled, loaded onto a truck and transported for disposal at a suitably permitted and licensed alternative treatment facility by way of incineration. Green Bins Randago Enterprises have developed safe working procedures to ensure the safe collection, transport and a safe system of work including but not limited to adequate supply and availability of receptacles, clear collection procedures and clear roles and responsibilities for all staff.
