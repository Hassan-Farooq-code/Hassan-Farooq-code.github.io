About Us

Green Bins Randago Enterprises is a gabage collection, Fumigation and Cleaning Services Company located in Kakamega County. With comprehensive experience and skilled environmental professionals, we provide high quality services teamed with a network of nationally recognised disposal facilities, to offer efficient, cost effective, environmentally sound solutions to garbage collection, Fumigation and Cleaning.

Mission

To provide society and our clients solutions and tools designed to implement environmental commitments in a safe, efficient and responsible manner.

Vision

To be a leader in the recovery and conservation of the environment, under our commitment to deliver comprehensive and permanent sustainable solutions to the environmental problems faced by our clients and society.

Why Greenbins

We provide technical support to our clients for waste disposal. We supply waste characterization and profiling support to clients as well as internal staff.

Ours Services

Our team of experienced professionals has performed hundreds of clean-up and garbage collection for private and public sector clents throughout the Northeast as a result of this experience, we have gained a unique knowledge and understanding of the diverse requirements and needs of a broad customer base.
